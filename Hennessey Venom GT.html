Hennessey Venom GT

On January 21, 2013, the Venom GT set a Guinness World Record for the fastest production car from 0 to 186 miles per hour (0 to 300 km/h) with an average acceleration time of 13.63 seconds.In addition, the car set an unofficial record for 0 to 200 mph (0 to 322 km/h) acceleration at 14.51 seconds, beating the Koenigsegg Agera R's time of 17.68 seconds, making it the unofficial fastest accelerating production car in the world.
